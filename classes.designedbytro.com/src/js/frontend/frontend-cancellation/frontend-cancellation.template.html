For Intro to Clay classes:

  • Because of the very small class size, a 50CHF deposit is due 45 days prior to the start of class to hold your seat. The remainder of the class fee is due on the first night.
  • If you must withdraw more than 45 days before the start, the deposit is fully refundable.
  • If you withdraw between 14 and 45 days before the start, the deposit is refundable less a 25chf administrative fee.
  • The deposit is nonrefundable for cancellations less than 14 days before the class start.

Workshops:

  • Workshop fees are due within 48 hours of registering in order to reserve your place.
  • If you withdraw more than 45 days before the workshop, the class fee is fully refundable.
  • If you withdraw between 7 and 45 days, the workshop is refundable less a 25chf administrative fee.
  • If you withdraw less than 1 week before the start of the workshop, the fee is nonrefundable.
